# AI COMMUNICATION INTELLIGENCE PLATFORM V2.0 – COMMUNICATION DNA ENGINE

## PRODUCT PHILOSOPHY
The platform is an AI Communication Intelligence Engine that interprets human behavior, communication effectiveness, confidence, leadership presence, engagement, and persuasion ability through multimodal AI analysis. Every feature answers: "How effectively is this person communicating?" rather than "What technical metrics were detected?"

## PRACTICE MODES
1. Interview Practice
2. Public Speaking Practice
3. Presentation Practice
4. Leadership Conversation Practice
5. Sales Pitch Practice
6. Custom Practice Mode

## ANALYSIS PILLARS

### 1. SPEECH INTELLIGENCE
**Analyze:** Speech Pace, Speaking Speed, Clarity, Filler Words, Strategic Pauses, Vocal Variety, Confidence in Voice, Energy Levels, Tone Consistency
**Metrics:** Words Per Minute, Average Response Length, Speaking Speed Trend, Speech Consistency Score

### 2. GESTURE INTELLIGENCE (MediaPipe Hands)
**Analyze:** Open/Closed Hand Gestures, Hands Hidden, Hand Visibility, Gesture Frequency/Timing, Fidgeting, Face Touching.
**Meaning:** Open Gestures = Trust, Confidence. Closed Gestures = Nervousness, Defensiveness.

### 3. FACIAL INTELLIGENCE (Face Mesh)
**Analyze:** Eye Contact, Smile Frequency, Expression Variety, Facial Engagement, Attention Level, Emotional Consistency, Stress Indicators.
**Metrics:** Eye Contact Score, Engagement Score, Facial Confidence Score, Emotional Stability Score.

### 4. POSTURE INTELLIGENCE (Pose Detection)
**Analyze:** Upright Posture, Head Position, Shoulder Alignment, Stability, Body Movement, Leaning, Excessive Swaying.
**Metrics:** Posture Score, Stability Score, Executive Presence Score.

### 5. CONTENT INTELLIGENCE (GPT Analysis)
**Analyze:** Communication Structure, Clarity, Storytelling Ability, Persuasiveness, Relevance, Professionalism, Confidence of Content, Logical Flow.
**Metrics:** Clarity, Storytelling, Persuasion, Professionalism, Structure Scores.
